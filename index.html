TOGAF Study Tips

Master TOGAF with these essential strategies:

  • Always start with Stakeholder Analysis - Identify who has concerns
  • Use Architecture Vision - Document and communicate the direction
  • Apply Risk Assessment - Identify, classify, and mitigate risks
  • Leverage Architecture Views - Present alternatives and trade-offs
  • Ensure Governance - Use Architecture Board and compliance reviews
  • Plan Incrementally - Phase implementation to manage complexity
  • Coordinate Frameworks - Align with business planning and portfolio management
